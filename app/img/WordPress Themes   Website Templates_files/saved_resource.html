On What Phones Is the Wicon Virtual Wallet app available?

The Wicon Wallet app is available from the Google Play store for Android phones and tablets running Android 4.0 or above, and will be available from the App Store for iPhones and other iOS devices running iOS 7.0 or above.

What Is Wicon Virtual Wallet?

The Wicon Wallet app is designed to give you a fast, free way to send and request money from anyone. When you receive money through Wise Wallet, you can quickly cash out to your bank account using a debit card or linked bank account.

What Will Happen to My Gift Cards, Loyalty Programs, and Offers?

All gift cards, loyalty programs, and offers have been automatically transferred to the Wicon Wallet app on your device.

What if My Phone or Card Is Lost or Stolen?

If your phone is ever lost or stolen, you can instantly remove access to your account on that phone at info.company.com.

What Is Wicon Wallet Protection?

Wicon Wallet Fraud Protection covers 100% of verified unauthorized Wize Wallet transactions reported within 120 days of the transaction. Protect your card data with passcode or fingerprint. Wicon Virtual Wallet comes with 24/7 fraud monitoring and covers 100% of all verified unauthorized transactions.

How Does Wicon Wallet Protect My Payment Info?

Wicon Wallet stores your data on secure servers and encrypts your payment information with industry-standard SSL (secure socket layer) technology. Your full credit and debit card information is never shown in the app. We also protect access to your Wicon Wallet with your PIN. We recommend locking your phone with a passcode for additional security.
